ABOUT US

Our journey started nine years ago when our first restaurant opened its doors in Nanyuki. With that flagship store, we set out to bring to life a series of high-quality Pizza lounges that offer not only delicious pizza and exceptional service, but unparalleled ambiance too.
Today, we have 4 stores across Kenya, and are one of the only brands to achieve ‘Superbrand’ status in East Africa. More than a restaurant, Pizza Lounge is a lifestyle and a social hub in the buzzing capital of Laikipia County.
We are firm believers that dining is about so much more than food. We combine inspirational spaces, quality products, and a sense of community to give you the full experience.
